MarkPad Privacy Policy

The latest version of the MarkPad Privacy Policy is always available here: http://code52.org/MarkPadRT/privacy.html

MarkPad makes use of the following libraries
  • MarkDownDeep - for converting Markdown to HTML
  • MVVMLight - for model-view-model structure

Information Collected

Your IP addresses is recorded and logged everywhere on the Internet. MarkPad does not sell nor trade these logs.

Sharing Data

MarkPad allows you to share data with third party applications such as Mail or social networks. Such information sharing is at your own discretion and will always involve your approval. Your use of any third party applications or services is governed by those applications/services individual privacy policies.
